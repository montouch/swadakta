Payments & Pricing

Quote first. Provider-held money. Release by proof.

Swadakta does not hold client money. Clients pay through payment providers such as Stripe, PayPal, M-Pesa, bank, Paystack, Flutterwave, or a regulated escrow/payment provider where needed. Swadakta earns the disclosed service fee in the quote, tracks evidence, and only moves milestones after proof review.

Money clarity

A quote is not one single bucket of money.

Swadakta separates ordinary service charges from protected or pass-through money. Clients should understand what is being paid, what is held for proof or milestones, and what cannot be treated as Swadakta income.

Client quote

The total amount a client is asked to pay for a clearly scoped job. The quote should name the service, proof standard, payment rail, milestone terms, and expiry date.

Service and operating fee

Swadakta's fee is included in the quote. It covers coordination, support, verification workflow, AI tooling, payment administration, proof review, and operating risk.

Provider-held / pass-through money

Supplier deposits, construction money, title/property money, family support money, and other pass-through amounts must stay with the payment provider, bank, regulated escrow route, supplier, or named recipient. Swadakta records the reference and milestone state; it does not hold those client funds.

Client-safe wording

What a payment note must say

1. What is being paid for

Scope, location, proof needed, expected timing, and what is excluded.

2. What is protected or pass-through

Any supplier, family, construction, title, or high-value money is named separately from service work and tied to a provider reference.

3. When money can move

Provider confirmation comes before paid work; milestone approval comes after proof review and points to the next provider action.

4. What Swadakta is not

Swadakta is not a bank, law firm, trustee, money-transfer business, or escrow agent. Money custody stays with the payment provider or regulated route.

Money custody boundary

Swadakta does not hold client money.

For high-value property, title, construction, supplier deposits, or legally sensitive jobs, use a regulated escrow/payment provider, payment processor, bank, or direct supplier/recipient route before receiver payout. Swadakta can track milestones and proof, but it is not currently a licensed escrow provider and should not pretend to be a bank, escrow company, money-transfer service, law firm, or tax advisor.
