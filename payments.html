Payment rails

Use the lowest-friction safe rail first.

Swadakta should not make the founder reconcile every payment manually. The normal order is card or PayPal for global clients, M-Pesa for suitable KES collections, bank transfer where a receipt trail is clean, and Wise only as an exception fallback.

Escrow boundary

Swadakta is not currently a licensed escrow provider.

For high-value property, title, construction, supplier deposits, or legally sensitive jobs, use a regulated escrow/payment provider or staged provider-held funds before receiver release. Swadakta can track milestones and proof, but should not pretend to be a bank, escrow company, law firm, or tax advisor.
