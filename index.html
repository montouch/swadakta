Workflow

Every request becomes a tracked mission.

  1. 01

    Brief

    Capture the client goal, location, deadline, documents, and risk points.

  2. 02

    Verify

    Confirm contact details, office requirements, contractor claims, and access needs.

  3. 03

    Execute

    Visit, collect, coordinate, source, follow up, or manage the assigned local task.

  4. 04

    Report

    Deliver media, receipts, notes, next steps, and a plain-English recommendation.
