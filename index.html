Trust and payments

Clear boundaries before real money moves.

Swadakta is quote-first and proof-first. AI can help draft, explain, and route, but it cannot verify ID, release money, assign paid work, mark payment received, or clear legal/customs risk by itself.

ID checks before paid work

Provider verification is required for receiving work and sensitive/high-value tasks.

Provider rails before payout

Stripe, PayPal, M-Pesa, bank, or approved providers confirm funds before milestones move.

Issues stay visible

Delays, proof gaps, refunds, disputes, and restricted goods go through the resolution flow.

Ready when you are

Open one account and choose your path.

You can start as a client, a job seeker, or both. Verification is not needed to open an account, but it is required before paid posting, paid receiver work, and sensitive jobs.
