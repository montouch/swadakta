Questions

What clients should know before sending a job.

Do you replace lawyers or surveyors?

No. Swadakta handles coordination, verification, errands, and reporting. Legal, valuation, and survey work should be handled by licensed professionals.

Can you hold money for projects?

Not by default. We can verify progress and coordinate payments, but client funds should move through agreed payment channels with clear receipts.

Can you contact relatives or contractors?

Yes, if the client gives permission and shares the correct contact details. Sensitive situations are handled carefully and documented.

What happens if a task cannot be completed?

The client receives the issue, evidence gathered, time spent, and recommended next steps before more work is approved.
